Our Story

Our story began with two simple loves: cats and coffee. Over the years, we found ourselves drawn to cat cafés around the world—warm, calming spaces that instantly felt special.

Every visit sparked the same question: why doesn’t Karachi have something like this? Cats have always been part of our homes. Having “too many cats” was never an issue—it was normal for us.

"This is a family owned cafe of 3 young women and a teenager boy, who turned a shared idea into a dream we couldn’t ignore."
