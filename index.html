Our story began with two simple loves:
cats and coffee.

Over the years, we found ourselves drawn to cat cafés around the world—warm, calming spaces that instantly felt special. Every visit sparked the same question: why doesn’t Karachi have something like this?

Cats have always been part of our homes. Having “too many cats” was never an issue—it was normal for us. Turning that lifelong love into something we could share felt like the most natural step.

"This is a family owned cafe of 3 young women and a teenager boy, who turned a shared idea into a dream we couldn’t ignore. Our goal was simple: to create a cozy space Karachi was missing, where good coffee, comfort food, and friendly cats come together."

This café is our love letter to the city, cats, and to anyone who needs a pause from the rush.
